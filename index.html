My name is Desiree Hicks I'm 19 years old and I'm from Long Beach California. I attended Lakewood High School in Lakewood, Ca. While attending Lakewood High School, I was accepted into a CNA program through Goodwill SOLAC which only ten students from each high school were chosen. I took interest in CNA because during my sophomore year my uncle, who was like my father, passed away due to cancer. Seeing my uncle being cared for in his home as he slowly drifted into his afterlife was what inspired me to pursue a career in nursing. The program was free for highschool seniors and only required you to attend one Saturday a month. After graduating high school I participated in nine weeks of the CNA program and graduated in August of 2018 with my license as a Certified Nurses Assistant.
